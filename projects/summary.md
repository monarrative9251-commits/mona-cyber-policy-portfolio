# Change Healthcare Ransomware Attack (2024)

## Overview
In 2024, Change Healthcare was targeted by a ransomware attack that disrupted claims processing, pharmacy transactions, and payments across the U.S. healthcare system.

## Key Issue
The incident exposed a structural risk: heavy reliance on a single vendor for critical healthcare operations.

## Policy Gap
Existing regulations focus on data privacy (e.g., HIPAA) but do not address vendor concentration or operational resilience.

## Policy Direction
- Limit vendor concentration in critical services
- Require redundancy and contingency planning
- Strengthen oversight of critical service providers

## Why It Matters
Cybersecurity risk in healthcare is not only technical—it is systemic. Vendor concentration can turn a single breach into nationwide disruption.
